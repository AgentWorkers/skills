## ⚙️ 无脸视频的创作公式

```
PROVEN FACELESS VIDEO STRUCTURES:

Format 1 — "Top N" List
  Example: "10 Countries You Can Move to for Under $1,000/Month"
  Why it works: Clear value promise, easy to produce, high completion rate

Format 2 — "Hidden Truth" Reveal
  Example: "The Dark Side of Working at Amazon Nobody Talks About"
  Why it works: Curiosity gap + controversy = clicks + comments

Format 3 — "Before vs After" Story
  Example: "What $100,000 Looks Like Invested for 30 Years"
  Why it works: Transformation hook + educational value = saves + shares

Format 4 — "Warning" / "Mistake" Frame
  Example: "7 Investing Mistakes That Are Keeping You Poor"
  Why it works: Loss aversion is the strongest human motivator

Format 5 — "Step by Step" Tutorial
  Example: "How to Start Dropshipping With $0 in 2026 (Exact Steps)"
  Why it works: High search intent, how-to content dominates YouTube SEO

Format 6 — "Comparison" / "Ranked"
  Example: "Every Streaming Service Ranked: The Honest Truth in 2026"
  Why it works: High search volume, easy to update, evergreen
```

## ⚙️ 完整的工作流程

```
INPUT: Niche + channel goal + monthly video target
        ↓
STEP 1 — Niche Intelligence Scrape
  └─ Top 50 performing videos in niche last 90 days
  └─ Extract: view count, title structure, video length, hook type
  └─ Identify: what formats work best in this niche?
        ↓
STEP 2 — Keyword Gap Detection
  └─ Google Search: high-volume keywords with under-served YouTube content
  └─ "Low competition" = top results have under 100K views
  └─ Prioritize: evergreen topics over trending (consistent views)
        ↓
STEP 3 — Viral Title Formula Extraction
  └─ Analyze title patterns of top 100 videos in niche
  └─ Extract: number formulas, power words, emotional triggers
  └─ Build: niche-specific title template library
        ↓
STEP 4 — 30 Video Ideas Generation
  └─ Mix: 20 evergreen + 8 trending + 2 high-competition swings
  └─ Each idea: title (3 variations) + angle + target keyword
  └─ Rank by: estimated search volume × competition gap
        ↓
STEP 5 — Script Writing (30 scripts)
  └─ Hook: first 30 seconds must create a "loop" the viewer must close
  └─ Structure: problem → agitation → solution → proof → CTA
  └─ Length: optimized per format (8–12 min for monetization)
  └─ SEO: target keyword in first 30 seconds naturally
        ↓
STEP 6 — InVideo AI Produces All 30 Videos
  └─ AI voiceover: natural, engaging, human-sounding
  └─ Visuals: stock footage matched to script sections
  └─ Captions: auto-generated, accuracy-checked
  └─ Music: royalty-free background matched to tone
  └─ Export: MP4 + thumbnail template per video
        ↓
STEP 7 — 30-Day Upload Calendar
  └─ Upload frequency: daily or every 2 days
  └─ Best upload times per niche
  └─ First 10 videos: foundational evergreen content
  └─ Videos 11–20: trending angle + evergreen mix
  └─ Videos 21–30: double down on what's working
        ↓
OUTPUT: 30 video ideas + 30 scripts + 30 produced videos + upload calendar + monetization projection
```
